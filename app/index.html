Beginning of an Outbreak

Preventing COVID19 in Community

This pilot project aims to capture some of the initial actions taken in community settings by businesses, schools, and other public places to mitigate the spread of COVID19. Students and faculty/staff from the College of Public Health posted pictures of public health measures they say during Spring Break 2020. This map illustrates both how far students may travel during Spring Break as well as the different public health messages employed by organizations. This map and the associated padlet data informs us about the spread of individuals, and the environments they encountered during the early phase of COVID19 in the US.
